Support Our Mission

Your donation helps us train service dogs that transform the lives of sexual assault survivors, providing healing, independence, and renewed confidence.

Make A Donation

When you donate to Valiant Heart Companions, you're directly supporting:

  • Specialized training of service dogs for trauma recovery
  • Matching dogs with survivors in need
  • Ongoing support for service dog teams
  • Public education about trauma recovery and service dogs

Your generous contribution, no matter the size, makes a significant impact in helping survivors reclaim their lives.

Other Ways to Support

Monthly Giving

Become a Valiant Heart Guardian with a monthly donation. Recurring gifts provide reliable support that helps us plan effectively for the future.

Corporate Matching

Many employers match charitable donations. Ask your HR department if your company offers matching gifts to maximize your impact.

Legacy Giving

Include Valiant Heart Companions in your estate planning to create a lasting legacy of healing and support for survivors.

In-Kind Donations

We welcome donations of supplies, equipment, and services. Please contact us to discuss your potential in-kind contribution.
